Project Overview

Our Kidney Stone Detection System represents a breakthrough in medical diagnostics, leveraging artificial intelligence to improve accuracy and efficiency in detecting urinary tract stones.

Our Mission

To provide accurate, accessible, and rapid kidney stone detection through AI-powered analysis, reducing diagnostic time and improving patient outcomes.

Technology

Utilizes a convolutional neural network (CNN) trained on thousands of annotated medical images with 95%+ accuracy in stone detection.

Clinical Impact

Reduces diagnostic time from hours to seconds, enables early detection of stones, and assists in treatment planning for urologists.

Collaborations

Working with leading hospitals and research institutions to validate and improve our detection algorithms across diverse patient populations.
